## 1. The Entropy Crisis (熵的危机)

### 1.1 The Trap of Type 0.7 (0.7 级文明的陷阱)
Humanity is currently a **Type 0.7 Civilization** on the Kardashev Scale. We rely on burning fossil fuels to power our cities and our silica.
However, our computational infrastructure is trapped in an "Entropy Spiral." 

To make a machine understand a simple "Hello," we squander 99% of our electrons merely on **"Data Logistics"**—fetching, decoding, predicting, and moving bits between CPU and RAM. The actual "calculation" (the processing of meaning) accounts for less than 1% of the energy consumed.

**We are not computing; we are just keeping the silicon warm.**

### 1.2 The Von Neumann Legacy & Evolution (冯·诺依曼的遗产与进化)
70 years ago, **John von Neumann** gave us the stored-program architecture. It was the optimal design for an era of expensive tubes and scarce memory, lighting the dawn of computing.

By separating "Storage" from "Compute," we created the **Von Neumann Bottleneck**. Every operation requires a round-trip across the bus. This is not just a performance bottleneck; it is an **Energy Wall**.
If AI continues to scale on this architecture, we will burn the planet before we reach the singularity.
